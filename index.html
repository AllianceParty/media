About Media Millennium

Media Millennium is a vibrant celebration of creativity and communication, bringing together students to showcase their talents across diverse formats.

From crafting reels in Reel Rush to producing podcasts in Radio Rally, participants engage with modern media tools, blending innovation with clarity to express ideas powerfully. This festival fosters collaboration, sharpens skills, and inspires students to explore the dynamic world of media.
